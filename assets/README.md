# Assets — drag your files into these folders

Drop images/files into the matching folder. Don't worry about naming or organizing —
I'll rename, optimize, and place them into the site for you.

| Folder | What goes here |
|---|---|
| `logos/` | The Flipped Garage logo (any format: PNG, SVG, JPG). If you have a light + dark version, drop both. |
| `photos/` | Shop photos, cars you've worked on, before/afters, the building, the team, tools, action shots. The more the better. |
| `brand/` | Anything showing your look: business cards, signage, a screenshot of a color you like, fonts, decals, wrap designs. |
| `inventory/` | Only if you plan to show products/parts for sale — product photos + a price/name in the filename or a note. Skip for now if unsure. |

Tips:
- Higher resolution is better — I can shrink, I can't enlarge.
- Phone photos are totally fine.
- If a photo has a story ("this is the Supra we did last month"), jot it in `context/notes.md`.
