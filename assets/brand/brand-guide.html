Usage Notes

  • Keep clear space around the badge equal to the height of the letter “F” in the wordmark.
  • The flipped car (wheels up) is the signature — never flip it back “right-side up.”
  • On dark backgrounds use the reversed (cream) versions; on light use ink or steel.
  • Steel-blue is an accent only — don’t recolor the whole logo unless using the steel variant intentionally.
  • Don’t stretch, add drop shadows, or place the badge on busy photos without a solid backing.
